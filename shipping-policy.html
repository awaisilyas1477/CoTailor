Shipping Policy

Last Updated: January 2024

1. Delivery Time

We aim to deliver all orders within 3 to 5 business days from the date of order confirmation. Delivery times may vary based on:

  • Your location
  • Product availability
  • Weather conditions
  • Public holidays

2. Delivery Charges

Standard delivery charges apply to all orders:

  • Delivery Fee: Rs 200 PKR
  • Delivery charges are calculated at checkout
  • Free delivery may be available for orders above a certain amount (check current promotions)

3. Shipping Areas

We currently ship to:

  • All major cities in Pakistan
  • Selected international locations (contact us for details)

4. Order Processing

Order processing timeline:

  • Orders placed before 2 PM are processed the same day
  • Orders placed after 2 PM are processed the next business day
  • You will receive a confirmation email with tracking details

5. Delivery Process

What to expect:

  • Our delivery partner will contact you before delivery
  • Please ensure someone is available to receive the package
  • Payment is collected upon delivery (Cash on Delivery)
  • Please inspect the package before accepting delivery

6. Delivery Issues

If you experience any delivery issues:

  • Contact us immediately at +92 303 4651965
  • Email us at info@cotailor.com
  • Provide your order number for faster resolution

7. Undeliverable Packages

If a package cannot be delivered due to:

  • Incorrect address
  • Recipient unavailable
  • Refusal to accept delivery

The package will be returned to us and a restocking fee may apply.
